A Little About Me

The Short Version

I'm Todd — a Wisconsin kid built on cold winters, three sports, and the unshakeable belief that the Vikings are going to win it all someday. I went to UW–Eau Claire, played college baseball, and somewhere along the way figured out that what I love most is leading people and building something worth being proud of.

I've worked in banking, fitness, and now eye care — different industries, same North Star: show up, do it right, treat people well. These days I'm proud to serve as General Manager at MyEyeDr in Vienna, VA, helping people see the world a little more clearly.

But honestly? My greatest title is Dad. Drake and Callie keep me on the pickleball court, on my toes, and grateful every single day.
